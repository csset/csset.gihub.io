Symposium Program

9:00am - 12:00pm Presentation & Judging
The presentation & judging are expected to begin at 9:00 am (EST) on Monday, April 26, 2021, We will have over 20 undergraduate and graduate students presenting their research and creative work. The project presentation will be judged based on Judging Rubric, and winning presentations will be announced on Tuesday April 27 before 5:00 pm (EST).

Every presentation has a channel in Computer Science Symposium on Emerging Technologies (CSSET) 2021 Team on Microsoft Teams. Presentations with embedded audio and posters will be available from 9:00 am on April 26, 2021 in each channel. From 9:00 am - 12:00 pm (EST) on April 26, presenters will engage in "synchronous commenting" in their presentation channel. Presenters will monitor and respond to questions and comments posted within their presentation channel in real-time. During this time period, judges will ask questions and evaluate presenters’ responses.
