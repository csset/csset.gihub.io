Symposium Program

Thursday, Apr 20, 2023

9:00am - 9:15am Welcome Note-- Dr. Tanvir Chowdhury
9:15am - 10:15am Oral Presentation
Oral presentation will take place from 9:15am to 10:15am in room: TBD.
10:15am - 11:55am Poster Presentation
The poster presentation & judging are expected to begin at 10:15 am (EST) on Thursday, April 20, 2023, We will have over 20 students presenting their research and creative work. The project presentation will be judged based on Judging Rubric, and winning presentations will be announced on Thursday April 20 before 5:00 pm (EST).

Every poster and presentation will be presented in Arthur Weisberg Family Applied Engineering Complex. From 10:150 am - 12:00 pm (EST), presenters will present their poster in front of judges. During this time period, judges will ask questions and evaluate presenters’ responses.
11:55am - 12:00pm Closing Note-- Dr. Tanvir Chowdhury
