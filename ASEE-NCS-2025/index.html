About ASEE North Central Section Conference

The ASEE North Central Section (NCS) cordially extends an invitation to authors in the fields of engineering, engineering education, computer science, and engineering technology to submit their workshop proposals, short and full-length papers, as well as student posters for presentation at our upcoming conference. This esteemed gathering, the 2025 ASEE NCS Conference, will be proudly hosted by Marshall University's College of Engineering and Computer Science in beautiful West Virginia, from March 28th to 29th, 2025. Join us in fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence in education and research within our vibrant community. We eagerly anticipate your participation and contribution to our conference proceedings.
