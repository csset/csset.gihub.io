Conference Venue

The conference will be held in the Arthur Weisberg Family Applied Engineering Complex building on the Marshall University campus. The venue is located 1.0 mile from the recommended hotels and downtown and is easily accessible on foot. You can find the Marshall University campus map at: https://www.marshall.edu/map/

Recommended Accommodation

All authors and participants are strongly recommended to stay at either the DoubleTree by Hilton, Huntington, or the Delta Hotel by Marriott, Huntington. These hotels are located just a 2-minute walk apart from each other. Both are centrally located in Huntington, steps from Pullman Square and within a 10-minute walk of the Mountain Health Arena. This upscale area is also 0.6 miles (1 km) from Marshall University and 1 mile (1.7 km) from Ritter Park.
